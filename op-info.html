Op. Info

Sledge

Sledge uses his sledgehammer to destroy walls and doors. This creates new lines of sight and entry points.

Thatcher

Thatcher’s throwable gadget temporarily disables nearby enemy equipment within its explosion range.

Ash

Ash is a fast and aggressive operator. Ash has a launcher that can rapidly breach destructible surfaces from a distance.

Thermite

Thermite's hard breaching charges create large openings in steel walls. Regular hard breaching charges create smaller openings. Soft breaching charges cannot breach steel walls.

Twitch

Twitch operates a two-wheeled ground drone. It can damage enemies and gadgets within a short range.

Montagne

Montagne’s shield can expand to provide full frontal coverage. Once expanded, his shield protects him from melee attacks. This feature is not found in shields used by other operators.

Glaz

Glaz’s marksman rifle has a unique scope with thermal vision that highlights enemies in yellow and can switch to a regular close-quarters sight.

Fuze

Fuze's gadget, the cluster charge, is placed on walls and upper floors. It fires multiple mini grenades horizontally.

IQ

IQ’s unique wrist-mounted gadget can display enemy equipment through walls.

Smoke

Smoke’s gas grenade inflicts damage and impairs operator vision.

Mute

Mute’s jamming device disables enemy equipment within a limited radius range.

Castle

Castle can deploy armored, bulletproof barricades on doors and windows to delay attacker progress.

Pulse

Pulse’s heartbeat sensor detects attackers through surfaces within a limited range.

Doc

Doc’s stim shot gadget can heal/revive himself or his teammates, regardless of distance.

Rook

Rook deploys a bag of armor on the ground for his teammates to collect, increasing their health bars.

Kapkan

Kapkan is equipped with a laser-activated explosive that is triggered by movement.

Tachanka

Tachanka is armed with an incendiary grenade launcher that damages or blocks enemy attackers.

Jaeger

Jaeger’s Active Defense System (ADS) fires at nearby enemy projectiles within a short range.

Bandit

Bandit’s Crude Electrical Device (CED) electrifies metal walls, destroying enemy equipment in contact.
