Op. Info

Sledge

Sledge is a soft breacher and flanker, he excels at creating new lines of fire and entry points, harassing the defenders.

Thatcher

  • The copper spool builds up the EMP by spinning.
  • LED gauge brightens when activated.
  • Twist top 45 degrees to activate the device.

Ash

Ash is a fast-paced front-liner; capable of breaching, flanking and dividing the Defender's attention in seconds.

Ash is capable of ranged soft breaching thanks to her modified M120 CREM Launcher.

Thermite

Thermite is one of the only hard-breachers in the game, losing Thermite early in the round will hinder your team significantly. Playing Thermite requires patience, leadership, and strong communication with your team.

Twitch

Twitch is a disabler and intel Operator. She can utilize these skills as either a remote back-line support or as an aggressive entry fragger.

Twitch is capable of remote disabling through her unique ability: RSD MODEL 1 DRONE.

Montagne

  • Collapses into half its size, providing increased mobility to its wielder.
  • Composite of reinforced alloys and Kevlar.
  • Provides protection against small-arms fire and close combat attacks.

Glaz

Glaz is a back-line marksman, capable of soft breaching and shooting from a far distance.

Glaz is capable of ranged shooting through his unique scope ability: HDS Flipsight.

Fuze

Fuze is best played as an aggressive flanker and area denial Operator. His strengths allow him to dispatch defensive capabilities and harass anchored enemies.

Fuze's APM-6 cluster charge propels a group of explosive cluster grenades through any soft and hard surface.

IQ

  • Device detects enemy equipment for EOD.
  • Wrist-mounted device that can be used on the fly.

Smoke

  • Toxic gas that causes damage and disoriented vision.
  • Double-sided tape allows vertical or horizontal mounting.

Mute

  • Jamms enemy equipment.
  • Device can be only be planted on horizontal flat space.

Castle

Castle is an anchor and secure operator, capable of creating defensive strongholds around the objective, controlling the flow of the attackers, and slowing down their assault.

Castle's unique ability is to create reinforced barricades using the:
UTP1-Universal Tactical Panel.

Pulse

Pulse is a roamer and intel operator for a defending team. Thanks to his HB-5 Cardiac Sensor, Pulse can track attackers through surfaces, gathering valuable intel.

Doc

  • Handheld, trigger-operated loading device.
  • 1mg dose of epinephrine, in a saline solution.
  • The loading device is powered by a pressurized CO2 canister.

Rook

Rook is an anchor and buff operator, capable of providing a boost to the defenders.

Rook is capable of protecting himself and his team mates through his unique ability: R1N ""RHINO"" Armor.

Kapkan

Kapkan is trap operator, equipped with an Entry Denial Device (EDD-MK II).

This trap is a packed C4 charge activated when motion is detected. It can be placed on door and window frames.

Tachanka

Tachanka’s new gadget, the Shumikha Launcher, is perfect for area denial and for covering unfavorable angles. Its heavy incendiary grenades can bounce multiple times, and their fuse timer only starts after the first bounce, giving them a lot of versatility.

Tachanka is equipped with 10 grenades at the beginning of a match and the Launcher can hold up to 7. For maximum effectiveness, use several grenades together to cover a wider area and prevent your opponents from avoiding them. Once launched, you’ll only be able to reload one at a time.

Jaeger

Jaeger can help his team create a defensive stronghold before roaming the map to intercept flanking attackers. He is capable of destroying incoming projectiles due to his unique deployable gadget: the Active Defense System or ADS.

Bandit

Bandit is a secure, anti-hard breacher and roamer, capable of denying key entry points for the attackers and defending the objective while on the move.

Bandit is capable of electrifying a variety of surfaces thanks to his special ability: Crude Electrical Device or CED-1 Shock Wire.
