Op. Tips

Sledge

Sledge excels at quietly and quickly breaching destructible surfaces. His sledgehammer is significantly quieter than traditional breaching charges.

Thatcher

Thatcher excels at temporarily disabling enemy devices protecting metal walls, aiding in teammate breaches.

Ash

Ash excels at breaching from a distance. She solves the problem of vulnerability when placing a breaching charge, requiring a lengthy time frame.

Thermite

Thermite excels at quickly destroying metal walls, creating improved lines of sight and additional entrances.

Twitch

Twitch excels at destroying defender gadgets. She is less team-oriented compared to Thatcher. Twitch’s drone is best used to destroy cameras in the planning phase.

Montagne

Montagne excels at blocking enemy access to key areas and effectively provides teammate cover.

Glaz

Glaz excels at long-range engagements. His smoke grenades provide an advantage, as his thermal vision scope allows him to see through smoke while remaining hidden from enemies.

Fuze

Fuze's gadget is most effective when deployed above the objective from various locations. Using breaching charges strategically with his cluster charges can significantly increase the enemy's vulnerability.

IQ

IQ excels at detecting hidden cameras. She is a strong counter to defending operators who heavily rely on their gadgets.

Smoke

Smoke’s gas grenade are most effective when used inside or near the objective. These grenades can be strategically deployed to block key areas from attackers. This is especially true in the final moments of a round.

Mute

Mute’s jamming devices can be strategically deployed to prevent attackers from breaching important walls and ceiling areas.

Castle

Castle’s armored barricades are effective when deployed on doors. The design of these barricades allows for the operators’ feet to be visible underneath, enabling damage to be inflicted from a safe distance.

Pulse

Pulse is skilled at roaming in proximity to the objective. His sensor assists in communicating the location of enemy attackers.

Doc

Doc excels at defending the objective room closely. It is crucial to administer the stim shot to an injured teammate when they are stationary. Additionally, Doc’s ability to self-heal becomes vital when he is the last remaining teammate.

Rook

Rook’s bag of armor should be placed down as soon as the round starts.

Kapkan

Kapkan’s laser traps are best positioned at or near key entry points that attackers are likely to use.

Tachanka

Tachanka excels at being located in the objective room. His launcher functions similarly to Smoke’s gas grenade but does not block vision.

Jaeger

Jaeger’s gadget should be deployed in a safe spot, within the objective room. It can be destroyed easily if placed on a destructible wall.

Bandit

Bandit’s device is a prime target. If attackers try to disable it, stay close to electrified walls. Walls can quickly be re-electrified with a spare device.
