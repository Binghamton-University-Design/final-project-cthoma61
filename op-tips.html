Op. Tips

Sledge

Sledge is very useful for a quick way to get through a barricade. Breaching charges are significantly louder comapred to his breaching hammer.

Thatcher

Thatcher is ideally used to help with hard breachers. Mostly used to temporaliy take out Bandit, Mute, and Kaid gadgets, to breach.

Ash

Ash is great for breaching from a distance. Ash solves the problem of being vulnerable when breaching.

Thermite

Thermite is great for hard breaching through walls. Ideally he should use both breaches as fast as possible in the round.

Twitch

Twitch’s drones are great for taking out defender gadgets. She is more of a secondary use to Thatcher. Her drones are best used against cameras in the planning phase. 

Montagne

Montagne is a shield operator. His shield can extend out so that when he stands, his legs cannot get shot.

Glaz

Glaz is great at long distances. It is best to use smoke grenades because Glaz can see through, but enemies cannot see him. 

Fuze

Fuze uses a charge that goes through any wall and shoots grenades through. It is best to use him above the objective if possible.

IQ

IQ is best for locating hidden cameras. She is a big counter to Pulse and Vigil.

Smoke

Smoke is great for being inside the objective. He is able to block off multiple doors, which is crucial in the last few seconds of a round.

Mute

Mute’s gadgets are best used on reinforced walls. They are great at stoping hard breachers.

Castle

Castle’s barricades are great at slowing down attackers. He is best used on doors so operators can peek under and hurt their feet.

Pulse

Pulse is best used for marking where the enemies are. Team communication is key.

Doc

Doc is best used in the objective. It’s important to shoot Doc’s stim while the teamate is still. Doc is best at self-healing, when he is the last teammate alive.

Rook

Rook’s plates should be placed down as soon as the round starts.

Kapkan

Kapkan’s traps are best used at key entry points or close to key entry points.

Tachanka

Tachanka is best used inside the objective. His launcher works similar to smoke’s canisters.

Jaeger

Jaeger’s gadget should be placed in the objective and somewhere safe from destrution. It can be destoyed easily if on a breachable wall.

Bandit

Bandit’s shock charges are used on reinforced walls. It’s best to stand by them if they get destroyed or temporarily disabled to recharge the walls.
