Op. Tips

Sledge

Sledge is very useful for a quick way to get through a barricade. He is also quite silent compared to averge attacker when breaching in.

Thatcher

Thatcher is ideally used to help with hard breachers. Mostly used to take out Bandit, Mute, and Kaid gadgets, temporaliy to breach.

Ash

Ash is great for breaching from a distance. Often times defenders can hear someone breaching close and shoot through.

Thermite

Thermite is great for hard breaching through walls. Ideally he should be use both breaches as fast as possible in the round.

Twitch

Twitch’s drone is great for taking out defender gadgets. She is more of a secondary use to Thatcher. She is best against cameras.

Montagne

Montagne is a shield operator. His shield can extend out so that when he stands his legs cannot get shot.

Glaz

Glaz is great at long distances. It is best to use smoke grenades so that Glaz can see through but enemies cannot see him.

Glaz

Fuze uses a charge that goes through any wall and shoots grenades through. It is best to use him above the objective if possible.

IQ

IQ is best for locating hidden cameras. She is a big counter to Pulse and Vigil.

Smoke

Smoke is great being inside the objective. He is able to block off multiple doors which is crucial in the last few seconds of a round.

Mute

Mute’s gadgets are best used on reinforced walls. They are great at stoping hard breachers.

Castle

Castle’s barricades are great at slowing down attackers. He is best used on doors so you can peek under and hurt their feet.

Pulse

Pulse is best used for marking where the enemies are. Communication to the team is key.

Doc

Doc is best used in the objective. It’s important to shoot Doc’s stim while the teamate is still.

Rook

Rook’s plates should be placed down as soon as the round starts.

Kapkan

Kapkan’s traps are best used at key entry points or close to key entry points.

Tachanka

Tachanka is best used inside the objective. His launcher is similar to smoke’s canisters.

Jaeger

Jaeger’s gadget should be placed on the ground behind cover. It can be destoyed easily if on a wall or is out in the open.

Bandit

Bandit’s shock charges are used on reinforced walls. It’s best to stand by them if they destroyed or emped to recharge the walls.
